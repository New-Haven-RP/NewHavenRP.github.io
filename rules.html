Community Rules

General Rules

  • Respect Everyone: Treat all members with respect. Harassment, discrimination, and hate speech will not be tolerated.
  • No Spamming: Avoid spamming messages, images, or links. Keep the channels clutter-free and relevant.
  • Follow the Law: Any activity that violates local, national, or international laws is prohibited.
  • Privacy Matters: Do not share personal information about yourself or others. Respect the privacy of all members.
  • No Impersonation: Do not impersonate other members, moderators, or administrators.
  • Appropriate Content: Keep all content shared appropriate for the community. NSFW content is strictly prohibited.
  • Racism in any form: We have a zero tolerance policy for any racism. No matter what ethnicity you come from, if you say slurs or anything you will be kicked and/or banned from the server.

Discord Rules

  • Follow Discord's Terms of Service: Adhere to Discord's Terms of Service and Community Guidelines.
  • No Self-Promotion: Do not promote your own server, streams, or content without permission from the moderators. (unless in the Self Promo section).
  • Respect Moderators: Listen to and respect the decisions of the moderators. If you have an issue, address it privately and respectfully.
  • Channel-Specific Rules: Follow the specific rules and guidelines provided in each channel’s description.
  • No Malicious Links: Do not share links that are harmful, misleading, or disruptive.
  • Use Appropriate Channels: Keep discussions in the relevant channels. Off-topic conversations should be moved to the appropriate channels.
  • Racism in any form: We have a zero tolerance policy for any racism. No matter what ethnicity you come from, if you say slurs or anything you will be kicked and/or banned from the server.

Roleplay Rules

  • Stay in Character: Maintain your character and roleplay narrative. Avoid breaking character unless necessary.
  • No Metagaming: Do not use out-of-character information to influence in-character actions.
  • No Powergaming: Avoid forcing actions on other players or creating overpowered characters.
  • Consent is Key: Ensure all participants agree to the scenario, especially for sensitive topics.
  • Roleplay Realistically: Keep actions within the realm of possibility and consistent with the roleplay setting.
  • Respect Roleplay Spaces: Do not disrupt or interfere with ongoing roleplays unless invited.
  • Racism in any form: We have a zero tolerance policy for any racism. No matter what ethnicity you come from, if you say slurs or anything you will be kicked and/or banned from the server.
