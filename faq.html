Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is New Haven RP?

    New Haven RP is a role-playing community designed for immersive, fun, and interactive FiveM RP.

  • How can I join New Haven RP?

    Visit our Discord and there will be a guide on how to join labeled "How To Join". It will guide you through the joining process.

  • What are the rules for participating?

    Our rules are outlined on the Rules page. Make sure to review them thoroughly before participating.

  • Is there a fee to join?

    Currently, joining New Haven RP is free. However, donations are welcome to help support the community.
